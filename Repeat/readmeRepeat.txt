Repeat
You are given a sequence of N numbers. The sequence could be consisted of a smaller subsequence repeated several times. Your task is to find the shortest subsequence that when repeated enough times is the same as the N numbers.

Input
Input is read from the console
The first line is consisted of N numbers
separated by spaces
Output
Output should be printed to the console
The subsequence of numbers on a single line
separate the numbers by spaces
Constraints
1 <= N <= 1 000 000
Time limit: 0.8s
Memory limit: 128 MB
